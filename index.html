MAKE TWEETS GREAT AGAIN

President Trump is known for his steady stream of tweets. This map shows the ratio of pro Trump and anti Trump tweets from Twitter users. Data comes from the Twitter API and the tweets sentiment is analyized using the Watson API.

Implimentation and Limitations

This was built using twitter api, watson api, d3 topojson.
Limited by the hashtags searched and by the current limitations of Watson to analyize sentiments.
